Who is this guy?

Hey, my name is Keenan — I’m a multifaceted designer who’s out to define and design the future. I wasn’t cut from the same cloth. I’m coming from a place of chaos, contemplation, and curiosity.

From music engineering and philosophy to b&w photography and visual arts, I’ve always chased ideas across mediums and disciplines.

In school, I was part of the fringe, the loud, the restless, and creatively unfiltered. That instinct to challenge, stand out, and reimagine still drives everything I make.

Where has he been?

After graduating from California State University, Long Beach, I moved to Japan. First to Kanazawa, then Tokyo. Two years of absorbing the rhythm, nuance, and community. Discovering how design shifts across culture, language, and location.

Now I’m back living in America, designing work that is open-minded for an open-ended world.
