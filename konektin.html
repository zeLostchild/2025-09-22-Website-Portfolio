summary
Konektin is a cryptocurrency wallet made for sharing. The concept is to make crypto into a currency, not an investment. It takes away the barriers of crypto and makes it easy to send crypto to your friends.

The brand is fun and not so serious. The color palette invoke a sense of excitement and youthfulness. The logo says progressive forward thinking. The website landing page primes the user the lifestyle they can live. And the app makes you feel your transactions are secure and you can trust you won’t lose a byte of crypto.
