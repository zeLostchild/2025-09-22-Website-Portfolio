summary
Part-time Philosopher is an Organic, ethically sourced coffee bean for remote-working coffee drinkers, studying college students, that value intellectual conversation, and aspire to live the modern bohemian lifestyle.

The brand reflects a cool organic, tone. The boutique style gives space for coffee drinker to truly admire their sophistication in taste and down to earth rejection of the excesses of modern day life.
